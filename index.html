Kelly & Lauren

Located in Bel Air, Maryland. We are two sisters that travel to your on-site location that specialize in hair and makeup artistry. We provide modern, updated and on-trend looks for your special day.

About Us

Makeup (Kelly)

With thirty years of experience in the industry as a cosmetologist. I began working at a salon for six years, and then I joined Nordstrom to pursue my passion for makeup artistry. Over several years, I had the wonderful opportunity to work for Bobbi Brown, Laura Mercier, Chanel, and MAC Cosmetics. I started my family and went to work at a prestigious salon, where I have now been for nineteen years. Throughout my career, I have always specialized in bridal makeup. My sister, who has followed in my footsteps, and we have come together as a team dedicated to being the best.

Hair (Lauren)

With eighteen years experience in the industry as a licensed cosmetologist. I began working at a salon in Annapolis. Quickly, I realized my deep passion for the art of hair. Over the years, I honed my skills , refining my expertise and nurturing a profound appreciation for the bridal industry. Experiencing the bride-to-be's "wow" moment as she looks into the mirror fills me with an indescribable sense of joy and passion. I have joined my sister on this journey, and together, we are a team dedicated to being the best.
